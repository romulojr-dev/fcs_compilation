\textbf{Given:}
\[
J_A = 0.3\ \mathrm{kg\cdot m^2}, \quad
J_B = 12\ \mathrm{kg\cdot m^2}
\]
\[
B_A = 1.5\ \mathrm{N\cdot m\!-\!s/rad}, \quad
B_B = 50\ \mathrm{N\cdot m\!-\!s/rad} 
\]
\[
N_A = 20, \quad
N_B = 80 
\]
\[
K = 600\ \mathrm{N\cdot m/rad}
\]

\textbf{Required:}
\[
G(s)=\dfrac{\Omega_B(s)}{T_m(s)}
\]

\textbf{Solution:}

Gear (kinematic) ratio:
\[
r \equiv \frac{\theta_A}{\theta_B}=\frac{N_B}{N_A}.
\]  \\

With \(N_A=20,\ N_B=80\) we get
\[
r=\frac{80}{20}=4,
\] \\

so the motor spins 4 times faster than the load:
\[
\omega_A = 4\,\omega_B,\qquad \theta_A = 4\,\theta_B.
\]

Reflection factor for inertias and viscous damping is \(1/r^2\). \\

Thus
\[
J_{B\to A} = \frac{J_B}{r^2} 
\]
\[
J_{B\to A}= \frac{12}{4^2} 
\]
\[
J_{B\to A}= \frac{12}{16} 
\]
\[
J_{B\to A}= 0.75\ \mathrm{kg\cdot m^2},
\]
\[
B_{B\to A} = \frac{B_B}{r^2} 
\]
\[
B_{B\to A}= \frac{50}{16} 
\]
\[
B_{B\to A}= 3.125\ \mathrm{N\cdot m\!-\!s/rad}.
\]

\[
K_{A} = \frac{K}{r} = \frac{600}{4} = 150\ \mathrm{N\cdot m/rad}.
\]

Equivalent inertia and damping at motor shaft:
\[
J_{eq} = J_A + J_{B\to A} 
\]
\[
J_{eq} = 0.3 + 0.75 
\]
\[
J_{eq} = 1.05\ \mathrm{kg\cdot m^2},
\]
\[
B_{eq} = B_A + B_{B\to A} 
\]
\[
B_{eq} = 1.5 + 3.125 
\]
\[
B_{eq} = 4.625\ \mathrm{N\cdot m\!-\!s/rad}.
\]

Equation of motion at motor shaft (Laplace domain):  
\[
T_m(s) = \big(J_{eq}s + B_{eq}\big)\,\Omega_A(s) + K_{A}\,\Theta_A(s).
\] \\

Using $\Theta_A(s)=\dfrac{\Omega_A(s)}{s}$ gives
\[
T_m(s) = \left(J_{eq}s + B_{eq} + \frac{K_{A}}{s}\right)\Omega_A(s).
\]

\[
\frac{\Omega_A(s)}{T_m(s)} = \frac{1}{J_{eq}s + B_{eq} + \dfrac{K_{A}}{s}}
\]
\[
\frac{\Omega_A(s)}{T_m(s)}= \frac{s}{J_{eq}s^2 + B_{eq}s + K_{A}}.
\]

From the gear ratio,
\[
\Omega_B(s) = \frac{\Omega_A(s)}{r} 
\]
\[
\Omega_B(s) = \frac{1}{4}\,\Omega_A(s).
\]

Final transfer function:
\[
G(s)=\frac{1}{4}\cdot\frac{\Omega_A(s)}{T_m(s)}
\]

\[
G(s)= \frac{1}{4}\cdot\frac{s}{J_{eq}s^2 + B_{eq}s + K_{A}}.
\]

Substitute the numeric values \(J_{eq}=1.05,\ B_{eq}=4.625,\ K_{A}=150\):
\[
G(s)=\frac{s}{4\big(1.05\,s^2 + 4.625\,s + 150\big)}
\]

\[
G(s)= \frac{s}{4.2\,s^2 + 18.5\,s + 600}.
\]

\[
\boxed{\;G(s)=\dfrac{s}{4.2\,s^2 + 18.5\,s + 600}\;}
\]

\]

\textbf{Required:}
\[
G(s)\;=\;\frac{\Theta_L(s)}{E_a(s)}.
\]

\textbf{Solution:}
\[
n_1=\frac{N_2}{N_1}=\frac{100}{40}=2.5
]\]
\[
n_2=\frac{N_4}{N_3}=\frac{90}{30}=3
\]
\[N=n_1 n_2=7.5 .
\]

\[
\Theta_L(s)=\frac{1}{N}\
\]
\[
\Theta_m(s)=\frac{2}{15}\,\Theta_m(s).
\]

Equivalent inertia on motor shaft
\[
J_{\text{eq}}=J_1+\frac{J_2}{n_1^2}+\frac{J_3}{N^2}
\]
\[
J_{\text{eq}}=4+\frac{6}{(2.5)^2}+\frac{10}{(7.5)^2}
\]
\[J_{\text{eq}}=4+\frac{6}{6.25}+\frac{10}{56.25}
\]
\[
J_{\text{eq}}=4+0.96+0.177777\overline{7}
\]
\[
J_{\text{eq}}=\frac{1156}{225}\ \text{kg\,m}^2
\]
\[
J_{\text{eq}}\approx 5.13778.
\]

Equivalent viscous damping on motor shaft
\[
B_{\text{eq}}=\frac{D_1}{n_1^2}+\frac{D_2}{N^2}
\]
\[
B_{\text{eq}}=\frac{60}{(2.5)^2}+\frac{120}{(7.5)^2} 
\]
\[
B_{\text{eq}}=\frac{60}{6.25}+\frac{120}{56.25}
\]
\[
B_{\text{eq}}=9.6+2.13333\overline{3}
\]
\[
B_{\text{eq}}=\frac{176}{15}\ \text{N\,m\,s/rad}
\]
\[
B_{\text{eq}}\approx 11.73333.
\]

A useful common-denominator form is
\[
J_{\text{eq}}s^2+B_{\text{eq}}s
=\frac{1}{225}\,\Big(1156\,s^2+2640\,s\Big).
\]

Mechanical balance at the motor shaft:
\[
T_m(s)=\big(J_{\text{eq}}s^2+B_{\text{eq}}s\big)\,\Theta_m(s).
\]

Electrical (Laplace) equation:
\[
E_a(s)=(R+Ls)\,I_a(s)+K_b\\Theta_m(s)
\]
\[
T_m(s)=K_t\,I_a(s).
\] \\

Eliminate \(I_a\):
\[
E_a(s)=\frac{R+Ls}{K_t}\,T_m(s)+K_b\,\Theta_m(s)
\]
\[
E_a(s)=\left[\frac{R+Ls}{K_t}\big(J_{\text{eq}}s^2+B_{\text{eq}}s\big)+K_b\right]\Theta_m(s).
\]

\[
\frac{\Theta_m(s)}{E_a(s)}=
\frac{1}{\dfrac{R+Ls}{K_t}\big(J_{\text{eq}}s^2+B_{\text{eq}}s\big)+K_b}.
\]

Using \(\Theta_L=\Theta_m/N=(2/15)\Theta_m\),
\[
\frac{\Theta_L(s)}{E_a(s)}
=\frac{2}{15}\cdot
\frac{1}{\dfrac{R+Ls}{K_t}\big(J_{\text{eq}}s^2+B_{\text{eq}}s\big)+K_b}.
\]

Substitute the numeric \(J_{\text{eq}},B_{\text{eq}}\) and clear fractions with the factorization above:
\[
J_{\text{eq}}s^2+B_{\text{eq}}s=\frac{1}{225}\big(1156s^2+2640s\big).
\]

\[
\frac{\Theta_L(s)}{E_a(s)}
=\frac{2}{15}\cdot
\frac{1}{\dfrac{R+Ls}{K_t}\cdot\frac{1}{225}\,(1156s^2+2640s)+K_b}.
\]

\[
\boxed{\;
\frac{\Theta_L(s)}{E_a(s)}
=\frac{30}{\displaystyle \frac{R+Ls}{K_t}\,\big(1156\,s^2+2640\,s\big)+225\,K_b}\; }.
\]

\[
J_\text{eq} = J_A + \left(\frac{N_A}{N_B}\right)^2 J_B + \left(\frac{N_A N_C}{N_B}\right)^2 J_C

B_\text{eq} = B_A + \left(\frac{N_A}{N_B}\right)^2 B_B + \left(\frac{N_A N_C}{N_B}\right)^2 (B_C + B)

E_a(s) = R I_a(s) + K_e \Omega(s)
